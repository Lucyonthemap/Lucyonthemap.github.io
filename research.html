Research

Current Projects

I’m currently working on machine learning applications for urban tree canopy mapping, fireline effectiveness modeling, and spatial decision support tools for wildfire management. My research integrates satellite imagery, high-resolution spatial data, and ecological theory to address pressing environmental challenges.

Datasets

  • Urban Tree Canopy Dataset (2025)
  • Global Burned Area (coming soon)

Collaborations

I collaborate with the U.S. Forest Service (USFS), City University of New York, University of Colorado–Denver, and other partners to model and map ecological data, develop open-source tools, and publish spatial products that support science-informed decision-making.

About Me

I am an Ecologist and spatial analyst at the U.S. Geological Survey. I am a recent graduate of the Master of Science in Environmental Sciences & Management program with a focus in geospatial studies at Cal Poly Humboldt. I have years of training in geospatial analysis and geographic applications with an emphasis on interdisciplinary analysis and software programming. My current research focuses on spatial analysis in land cover and land use change, conservation biology, urban ecology, and ecosystem services. \n I have nine years of professional experience working with geospatial and environmental data. I published my first publication as lead author in 2017, working with sylvatic plague management in Colorado's prairie-stepp ecosystems. Since then I have contributed to many other scientific publications and developed a series of geospatial products for clients.
